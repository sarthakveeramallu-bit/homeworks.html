Typography with Food

Food subtance consiting essentially of proteins, carbohydrates, fat, and other nutrients used in the body of an organism to sustain growth and vital processes and to furnish energy.
The absorption and utilization of food by the body is fundamental to nutrtion and is facilited by digestion.
Plants, which convert solare energy to food by photosynthsis, are the primary food source.

Animals that feed on plants often serve as sources of food for other animals.

Food is treated in a number of articles. For a description of the processes of absorption and utilization of food, see nutrtion, nutrtion, human, digestion, and digestive system, human.
